Personal Library & Archive

A curated collection of notes, problem sets, and expository papers created during my undergraduate and graduate studies at UNAM. My goal is to prevent these materials from getting "lost in history" and to provide useful resources for students currently facing similar topics.

Course Notes

Personal transcriptions and notes from various courses (Algebra, Analysis, Number Theory, etc). These documents reflect my personal understanding of the subjects at the time and often contain detailed proofs that were sketched in class.

Expository Papers & Projects
