Focus & Interests

My main research area focuses on Analytic Number Theory. I am particularly interested in the asymptotic behavior of arithmetic functions and the use of complex analysis tools to solve counting problems. More specifically, my interests can be divided into four main areas:

Complex Analysis

Riemann Zeta Function ($\zeta(s)$), Transcendental Equations, and Holomorphic Dynamics.

Number Theory

Sieve methods, distribution of prime numbers, and additive problems.

Combinatorics

Enumerative, Analytic, and Symbolic Combinatorics. Connections with generating functions.

Probability

Applications of probability to Analytic Number Theory, Random Matrices, and their unreasonable effectiveness.

Questions I'm interested in

  • How can the Circle Method be generalized?
  • What is the intrinsic relationship between probability and number theory?
  • How is the Siewert-Burniston method applicable in different contexts?
