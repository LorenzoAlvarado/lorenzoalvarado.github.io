LudoCiencias — Multidisciplinary Festival

LudoCiencias is a multidisciplinary outreach festival organized by the Faculty of Sciences as part of the welcome activities for the incoming Generation 2026 at UNAM. The event is designed to introduce new students to university life through interactive, playful, and educational activities that showcase the diversity of academic disciplines offered by the Faculty.

As part of this event, I participate by contributing interactive activities focused on mathematics, aimed at engaging first-year students through a hands-on and approachable perspective. These activities promote direct interaction with the audience and present mathematics as an accessible and enjoyable discipline within the broader academic environment of the Faculty of Sciences.
