CIENPiés Matemático — Science Itinerant Program

CIENPiés Matemático — Science Itinerant Program is a science outreach initiative that combines mathematics, science, and art through interactive activities designed for high school students from the National Preparatory School (UNAM). The program promotes curiosity and interest in mathematics by presenting scientific knowledge in an accessible and engaging way.

As part of its outreach activities, the Festival Matemático participates in CIENPiés Matemático by contributing hands-on workshops and interactive activities developed and conducted by members of the festival. Through this collaboration with the Institute of Mathematics (UNAM), the Festival Matemático supports the goal of presenting mathematics as a living, creative, and approachable discipline.
