Festival Matemático

The Festival Matematico is a science communication project of the Institute of Mathematics at UNAM (National Autonomous University of Mexico), aimed at bringing contemporary mathematics closer to society through interactive and inclusive activities.

The Festival is organized by academics, students, and community service participants, who spend several months conducting a seminar to design and test new proposals. As the festival approaches, volunteers (students and professors) are recruited and trained to lead all activities.

Objectives

  • Promote mathematical culture in society at large.
  • Replace negative preconceptions about mathematics with a positive and encouraging vision that stimulates learning.
  • Disseminate the contemporary view of mathematics as a living science with multiple challenges to solve and many applications.

The Festival is designed to take place in public spaces and to offer free activities so that anyone, regardless of age or educational background, can participate. The activities are related to various branches of mathematics and, in many cases, are linked to everyday life, highlighting that mathematics is more than commonly imagined.
